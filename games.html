Not only is this a splendidly fun and immersive gaming experience, but it is also an interactive story.

It recounts the events of March 23rd 2017. I was sitting in a peaceful shadowed glade in Leigh Woods, eating some fried chicken, when a marauding band of angry chickens attacked me. Their savagery shocked me, and I stumbled around, trying to defend myself. I couldn’t think of any possible reason WHY they had attacked me. I was minding my own business, enjoying the summer air. I had brought no weapons to the peaceful glade… Leaving me only one option. The fried chicken! That fated chicken would become my weapon...
